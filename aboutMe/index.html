Eric Denovellis is a graduate student studying computational neuroscience at Boston University. His research focuses on how the prefrontal cortex supports the underlying neural comptuations necessary to switch between contexts. His primary advisors are Daniel Bullock at Boston University and Earl Miller at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. You can find his CV here.
